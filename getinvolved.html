How to get involved!

The Drug Policy Alliance is the best resource for students or faculty to get involved in the fight for legal recreational and medicinal cannabis use. Visit this site for more information on the legalization and regulation of marijuana. You can sign up to be a member of the alliance or find an event near you that you would like to participate in. Get involved and fight the fight for the generations to come! We still don't know all the capabilities of marijuana use because of illegalities. We could be saving lives, reversing deathly illness and prevent the progression of disease.
