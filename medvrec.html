Medical vs Recreational

Many people support the use of medicinal cannabis but not recreational uses. The question is why? It is interesting to compare the benefits and disadvantages of already legal substances such as alcohol and tabacco with marijuana. One sees that most of the disadvantages that pose serious and dangerous threats all come from tabacco and alcohol use. A person could have never had a sip of alcohol in their life and die from their first alcohol experience from alcohol poisioning. Deaths related strictly to use of marijuana are unheard of. But deaths related strictly to the use of alcohol or tabacco products are astronomical. Marijuana use as a recreational drug should be monitored, safe and used in moderation (like all things). But it is important to acknowledge the hypocrisy of a community denying use of cannabis without denouncing the use of alcohol. Alcohol is exponentially more dangerous than marijuana. So why do we have this conversation of medical vs recreational? Marijuana should be as legal as alcohol is. No question 'bout it.
